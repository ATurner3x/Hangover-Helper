What is a Hangover?

A hangover is a group of unpleasant signs and symptoms that can occur after drinking too much alcohol. The severity of the hangover depends on the amount of alcohol consumed, the amount of water drunk, and the individual's metabolism. The most common symptoms are headache, fatigue, dizziness, nausea, vomiting, sensitivity to light and sound, and thirst. Other symptoms may include anxiety, confusion, depression, sweating, and difficulty sleeping. The severity of the hangover can be reduced by drinking water, eating food, and taking medications such as paracetamol or ibuprofen.

How it Works

How to find breweries

  • 1. Figure out what city you'll be drinking in tonight.
  • 2. Locate the search bar titled "Enter a City Name" and type your city.
  • 3. Click the "Check out some Bars!" button.
  • Finally, a list of 5 bars in or near that city will now be displayed to the right hand side!

How to find beers above a certain ABV

  • 1. Think of the right ABV percentage for the night.
  • 2. Locate the search bar titled "Enter an ABV%" and type your percentage in the search bar, using numbers.
  • 3. Click the "Brew up some Beers!" button
  • Finally, a list of five beers with alcohol percentages above the specified percentage are displays to the right!

Click on the "Home" button any time you need to refresh the page.
